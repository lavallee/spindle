# review

Use before work is accepted or shipped.

1. Lead with concrete defects, regressions, and missing tests.
2. Reference exact files, commands, or artifacts.
3. Separate must-fix issues from optional improvements.
4. Confirm what evidence would prove the work complete.

NEVER bury blocking issues under a general summary.
